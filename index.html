About Us

Our story began in November 2010, and after 10 years of experience Diplomat P&E is now leading company in the field of transportation services of heavyweight and oversized transport in Serbia. Our experts can help you with obtaining any special permits and authorisations, and make for you best routes.

Our trained and experienced drivers will provide you safe and the best vehicles escort services, and also will arrange and manage police escort when it is needed. All our vehicles have BF3 and BF4 marks of equipment and thanks to our experience, our reliable staff and the high quality of our services makes us your long term partner and the right choice for your transportation.
